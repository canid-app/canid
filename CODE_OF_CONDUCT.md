# Code of conduct

canid exists so people from a lot of different scenes and communities can
share who they are on their own terms. The project space works the same way:
everyone is welcome whatever your identity, orientation, presentation,
communities, or experience level. Everyone is expected to be cool.

Being cool means:

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and reviews. Critique code, not people.
- No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or sexualized remarks directed
  at people.
- No outing anyone. Don't connect somebody's identities, handles, accounts,
  or communities without their say-so (in this project of all places).
- Assume good faith. Disagree plainly and kindly.

This applies in all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) and when
representing the project elsewhere.

If someone's behavior is a problem, email
[dev@canid.app](mailto:dev@canid.app). Reports are handled privately.
Depending on severity, consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban
from project spaces.
